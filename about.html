About Dolce Academy

Committed to excellence in electrology education.

Our Mission

The mission of Dolce Academy is to deliver high-quality education in Electrology. We offer the latest equipment available on the market, paired with skilled and knowledgeable instructors. At Dolce, we prepare our students to meet the expectations of the public and the most discerning employers, ensuring they become true professionals in the field. Upon completing the program, students will be eligible to apply for the Certified Professional Electrologist exam after one year of graduation.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy at Dolce is that we are creating a family. Our commitment extends beyond the 600-hour training; you will join a supportive community that is ready to answer your questions, provide additional training, assist with challenging clients, offer supplies at discounted rates, and present continuing education opportunities. We are here for you long after your 600 hours are completed!

Académie Dectro Partner School

academiedectro.com

Dolce Academy is partnered with Académie Dectro, who provides training to thousands of beauty professionals in hair removal and aesthetic care. Since 1978, Dectro International has designed and manufactured specialized devices intended for the global market of permanent hair removal and aesthetic care. Dectro International is now recognized as a leader in its field and distributes its products in more than 35 countries worldwide. Innovation and quality are key ingredients for Dectro. Dolce Academy teaches an electrolysis curriculum provided by Académie Dectro.
