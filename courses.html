Our Programs

ElectroClass Xtra
(600 Hours) Course

The curriculum consists of 600 hours of combined theoretical and practical instruction. Theory includes online coursework and study materials, while practical training occurs onsite. Students complete online modules at their own pace, with quizzes taken in person. The 300 hours of theory include 15 modules:

  • History
  • The Human Body
  • The Skin and Its Appendages
  • Ethics
  • Workstation
  • Consultation
  • Hygiene
  • Execution
  • Thermolysis
  • Electrolysis
  • Blend
  • Probes
  • Positioning
  • Marketing
  • Starting Your Business

Practical Hands‑On Portion Onsite (320 Hours)

The practical segment is conducted onsite at Dolce Academy. Training begins with orientation, distribution of student kits, and review of rules and procedures. Students practice on peers and volunteer models throughout their training.

  • Technique refinement
  • Advanced equipment use
  • Treatment strategy
  • Client management
